DK64 Randomizer Hint Viewer: Broadcaster Guide


First-time setup

1. Go to hintviewer.com and log in with your Twitch account
2. Upload your spoiler log JSON file using the upload button
3. The spoiler log is saved to the server; viewers can now see the ??? placeholders in the extension


During a run

Revealing a hint

Use the eye button next to any hint location to reveal it to viewers. Hints with the same text across locations are revealed together automatically. Use the Reveal All / Hide All buttons to bulk-toggle entire sections.

Marking a hint as completed

Once a hint is revealed, a checkmark button appears next to it. Click it to mark the hint completed and it will appear struck through for both you and viewers. Un-revealing a hint automatically clears its completed state.

Navigating levels

Use the carousel arrows or the Quick Nav sidebar to jump between level pages.


What viewers see

Viewers see ??? for unrevealed hints and the hint text for revealed ones. Completed hints appear with a strikethrough and reduced opacity. The extension auto-refreshes every 60 seconds; viewers can also hit the refresh button to update immediately.


Starting a new run

Upload a new spoiler log to replace the current one and reset all hints.
